data is seperated by :

0: Player:
x, y, size, speed, gravity, gravity direction


1: Goal:
x, y, size


2: Powers:
size, total displayable on screen
{\n seperation}
numbers seperated by , to indicate which powers are avaliable for the level

power types:
0 = up-left
1 = up
2 = up-right
3 = right
4 = down-right
5 = down
6 = down-left
7 = left
8 = clear (no collision required)


4: Blocks:
x, y, width, height, type
{each block seperated by \n}

block types:
0 = regular (green with basic collision)
1 = kill (red with kill collision)
2 = camera stops following player x
3 = camera stops following player y
4 = switch block
